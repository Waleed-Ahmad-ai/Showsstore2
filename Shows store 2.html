Our Heritage

Founded in 1924 in London's Mayfair, RegalFootwear has been synonymous with exceptional British craftsmanship for nearly a century. Each pair is still handcrafted in our Northampton workshop using techniques passed down through generations.

We source only the finest Italian leathers and British fabrics, ensuring every shoe meets our exacting standards of quality, comfort, and style. Our master shoemakers combine traditional methods with contemporary design to create footwear that stands the test of time.
